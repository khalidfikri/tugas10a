About our Profile

Mino is a technology company dedicated to designing and producing high-performance computers and laptops that cater to a wide range of users, from professionals and students to gamers and creatives. Established in 2015, Mino has rapidly grown into a trusted brand known for its balance between power, efficiency, and elegant design.


At Mino, we believe that technology should empower people, enabling them to achieve more without limitations. Our products are designed with cutting-edge hardware, seamless software integration, and sleek aesthetics to ensure that every device is not just a tool but an experience. Whether you need a lightweight ultrabook for mobility, a powerful workstation for intensive tasks, or a gaming machine that can handle the latest titles, Mino has the perfect solution.

History About us

Mino was founded in 2015 with a vision to create high-performance yet affordable computers and laptops for modern users. The company started as a small tech startup focused on designing compact and efficient computing solutions. Over the years, Mino has grown into a recognized brand, offering a diverse range of devices tailored for professionals, gamers, and students.


The Journey of Mino


2015 – The Beginning

Mino was established by a group of passionate engineers and designers who aimed to revolutionize the PC and laptop market. The company focused on creating lightweight yet powerful machines that balanced performance, aesthetics, and affordability.


2017 – The First Breakthrough

Mino launched its first product line, the Mino MinoBook Series, a series of sleek and lightweight laptops for students and professionals. The combination of stylish design and strong performance made it an instant success, helping Mino gain a foothold in the competitive tech industry.


2019 – Entering the Gaming Market

With the increasing demand for high-performance gaming laptops, Mino introduced the Mino MinoGS Series, featuring high-refresh-rate displays, dedicated GPUs, and advanced cooling systems. This launch solidified Mino’s reputation as a brand capable of delivering power-packed devices for gamers.


2021 – Expanding to AIO and 2-in-1 Devices

As more professionals turned to powerful computing solutions, Mino unveiled its MinoAIO Series, designed for developers, designers, and content creators. Around the same time, the Mino MinoFlip, a 2-in-1 convertible laptop with touchscreen capabilities, was introduced for users who needed flexibility and portability.


2023 – Pushing Boundaries with AI and Sustainability

Mino embraced artificial intelligence in its new lineup, integrating AI-powered battery optimization, intelligent cooling, and machine-learning-based performance enhancements. The company also took major steps towards sustainability by using eco-friendly materials and recyclable packaging.


2025 and Beyond – The Future of Mino

Mino continues to innovate, with plans to expand into cloud computing, AI-powered smart devices, and energy-efficient computing solutions. The company remains committed to its mission of delivering cutting-edge technology while maintaining affordability, reliability, and sustainability.

Our Mission in the Future

As technology rapidly evolves, Mino is committed to staying ahead of the curve by continuously innovating and improving our products to meet the demands of the future. Our mission is to redefine the computing experience by integrating advanced technologies, sustainable practices, and user-centric designs that empower people worldwide.


Key Goals for the Future


Cutting-Edge Performance

Mino aims to push the limits of computing power by integrating the latest processors, AI-driven optimizations, and next-generation graphics capabilities. Our goal is to create machines that can handle intensive workloads, from high-end gaming to professional content creation, with seamless efficiency.


Sustainability and Eco-Friendly Innovation

Mino is dedicated to reducing its carbon footprint by adopting green energy sources, recyclable materials, and energy-efficient components. Future laptops and computers will feature biodegradable packaging, lower power consumption, and modular designs for easy upgrades and repairs, extending product lifespans.


AI-Powered Smart Computing

We envision a future where AI plays a key role in enhancing user experience, optimizing system performance, improving security, and personalizing computing workflows. Mino will integrate AI-based cooling systems, predictive battery management, and adaptive performance tuning to make computing more intuitive.


Cloud & Connectivity Advancements

Mino is working towards seamless cloud integration, allowing users to access their files, applications, and workflows from anywhere with enhanced security and faster processing speeds. Future devices will feature 5G and Wi-Fi 7 support, ensuring faster and more stable connectivity.


Modular and Upgradeable Systems

To promote sustainability and affordability, Mino plans to introduce modular computing systems, allowing users to easily upgrade components such as RAM, storage, and GPUs without replacing entire devices. This approach will help reduce electronic waste and provide long-term value to consumers.


Next-Level Security

Cybersecurity threats continue to evolve, and Mino is committed to enhancing data protection through AI-driven threat detection, biometric security enhancements, and privacy-focused operating systems that safeguard user data.


Revolutionizing Gaming and Content Creation

Mino will continue to invest in high-performance gaming laptops and workstations, featuring real-time ray tracing, advanced cooling technologies, and AI-enhanced rendering to support next-gen gaming and creative workflows.


Our Vision for the Future

Mino’s future mission is to create powerful, intelligent, and sustainable computing solutions that not only enhance productivity and entertainment but also contribute to a greener and more connected world. By leveraging AI, cloud computing, and sustainable engineering, Mino is committed to shaping the future of technology while keeping it accessible to everyone.
