We are here for you

At Mino, our mission goes beyond just creating high-performance computers and laptops—we are dedicated to ensuring that every user gets the best experience, support, and innovation possible. Whether you’re a student, professional, gamer, or developer, we are here for you every step of the way.

Powered by Linux

Mino embraces the power, flexibility, and security of Linux as the core of its computing experience. By leveraging the open-source ecosystem, Mino delivers fast, efficient, and customizable devices tailored for developers, professionals, and power users who demand more control over their systems.


Why Linux?


Performance & Efficiency

Linux-based systems are lightweight and optimized for speed, reducing bloatware and unnecessary background processes. This results in faster boot times, lower resource consumption, and improved battery life on Mino devices.


Security & Privacy

With strong built-in security features, Linux is naturally resistant to malware and cyber threats. Mino enhances this by integrating biometric authentication, encrypted storage options, and firewall protection to keep your data safe.


Customization & Freedom

Unlike proprietary operating systems, Linux provides unmatched customization. Mino devices allow users to choose their preferred Linux distribution (Ubuntu, Fedora, Arch, or custom CreOS) and personalize their workflow with open-source tools and software.


Optimized for Developers & Power Users

Mino’s Linux-powered devices are designed for coders, engineers, ethical hackers, and tech enthusiasts who need a system that is lightning-fast, stable, and adaptable. Whether you’re running multiple VMs, compiling large codebases, or managing cloud servers, Mino ensures seamless performance.


Sustainability & Open-Source Support

By using Linux, Mino promotes sustainable computing by reducing dependency on closed ecosystems and embracing open-source innovation. We actively contribute to Linux kernel optimizations and community-driven projects to make computing more accessible and eco-friendly.
